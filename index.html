About Us

From chicken-loaded pizzas to fresh veggie delights, our menu offers something for every pizza lover. Baked on fresh dough and topped with a mix of cheesy and spicy goodness, it's a taste experience you won't forget. Add some crispy fries to make it a meal to remember!

Menu

Explore the wide range of pizza baked in fresh dough and vegetables. Our pizza will tempt all your excitement and senses. Made with a delicate balance of everything cheesy and spicy, you will get to savour the best pizza in town.

Pizza Mania

A true pizza lover’s dream, Pizza Mania offers a burst of flavors with a variety of toppings on a crispy, fresh-baked base. Perfect for satisfying your pizza cravings!

Mexicon Pizza

Spice up your day with our Mexicon Pizza, topped with zesty jalapeños, fresh veggies, and a hint of Mexican flair, all on a cheesy, golden crust.

Paros Special Pizza

Experience the ultimate indulgence with our Paros Special Pizza, loaded with premium toppings and rich, cheesy goodness. A unique creation that stands out!

Chicken Cheese Pizza

A delicious blend of tender chicken and gooey cheese, our Chicken Cheese Pizza is a savory treat that satisfies with every bite.

Panner Deluxe Pizza

Enjoy the fusion of Indian flavors with our Paneer Deluxe Pizza, topped with soft, marinated paneer cubes and a mix of fresh veggies, all on a cheesy base.

Mushroom Fresh Pizza

For mushroom lovers, this pizza is a must-try! Our Mushroom Fresh Pizza is loaded with sautéed mushrooms and melted cheese for a delightful, earthy flavor.
